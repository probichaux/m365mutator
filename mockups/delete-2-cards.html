Deletions

Delete a range of items across a user's workloads to test deleted-item recovery and retention. Choose the users, pick which workloads to touch, then scope the deletion by date.

Users

Accounts whose items may be deleted, as UPNs.

Workloads

Which data to delete from. Select one or more.

What to delete

Items are matched by the date they arrived — received date for mail, created date for calendar events and files. Boundary dates are included.
